Welcome to B&B "Au Mont des Collines"

In the prolongation of the Flemish Ardennes, between the Muziekbos (Ronse), D’Hoppe (Vloesberg) and the Brakelbos, you will find Ellezelles, also called the witches’ village. This is already Wallonia ! You’ll feel inmediately bewitched by the tranquil and resourcing countryside. Folklore, splendid nature, breathtaking vues, a warm welcome and heartfelt hospitality  are the unmistaken assets of this exceptional B & B.
